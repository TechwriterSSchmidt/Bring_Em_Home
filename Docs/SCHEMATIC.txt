Bring Em Home - Connection Schematic
=====================================
Hardware Platform: Heltec Mesh Node T114 (nRF52840)
Core: nRF52840 + SX1262 LoRa + L76K GNSS

External Components:
- 1.5" OLED Display (SH1107 Driver, 128x128, I2C)
- Bosch BNO055 9-Axis Absolute Orientation Sensor (I2C)
- Vibration Motor, High Power LED, External Button

GPIO Pin Summary (Arduino Pin Numbers):
=======================================
Pin Name     | Arduino Pin | nRF Pin | Function
-------------|-------------|---------|------------------------
PIN_BUTTON   | 42          | P1.10   | External Button (Active Low)
PIN_VIB      | 36          | P1.04   | Vibration Motor
PIN_LED      | 38          | P1.06   | Flashlight LED
PIN_NEOPIXEL | 40          | P1.08   | External WS2812 LED Data
PIN_VEXT     | 21          | P0.21   | Sensor Power Control
PIN_BAT_ADC  | 4           | P0.04   | Battery Voltage ADC
PIN_BAT_CTRL | 6           | P0.06   | Battery Divider Control (Active HIGH)
PIN_SDA      | 16          | P0.16   | I2C Data
PIN_SCL      | 13          | P0.13   | I2C Clock
